Overview

Desert Whisper is a creatively constructed, contemporary desert hideaway in the midst of the Namib Desert, inspired by nature and natural shapes, material and colours. This private retreat for 2 is situated in the Namib Desert Lodge, and offers stunning desert views, where the golden expanse of the Namib Desert extends to the horizon, fringed by the softness of the Naukluft mountains in the distance.

This intimate hideaway comprises 1 villa, comprising a spacious, single-storey suite with an open-plan kitchen, lounge and dining area, an en-suite bedroom with a balcony, and an outdoor area with braai facilities, as well as a private plunge pool. The bedroom area comprises a king-size bed and an en-suite shower, with spectacular desert vistas. The open-plan lounge and dining area has a well-stocked bar and a kitchen, with a fridge and an oven and a stove. Unlimited free Wi-Fi is available.

Soak up the scenery on the terrace with a glass of wine or cup of coffee, and stroll along the wooden walkway to take a dip in your private plunge pool. Embrace the solitude and magnificence of the Namib Desert at this unique desert retreat.
